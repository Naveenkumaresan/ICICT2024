ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

ICICT 2024


International Conference on Innovation in Computing & Technology (ICICT-2024) is aimed to promote the scientific platform for exchange of ideas between the researches and promoting innovation aspects in Engineering & Science. The conference will boost excellent innovations in terms of day life and academics. This International Conference on Innovation in Computing & Technology is Organized on 27 th MARCH 2024 by Department Of Computer Science and Engineering,Velammal Institute of Technology, Chennai, India. This Conference is an initiation to build a technical society for future collaboration and specific goal of globalization to achieve international standards.
